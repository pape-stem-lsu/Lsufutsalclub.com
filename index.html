Who We Are

More Than a Club

LSU Futsal Club is a student-run organization at Louisiana State University dedicated to growing the beautiful game of futsal in Baton Rouge. Whether you're a seasoned player or brand new to the sport, everyone is welcome.

We compete in intramural futsal and soccer leagues and host open play sessions — all while building a tight-knit community of passionate athletes who bleed purple and gold.
